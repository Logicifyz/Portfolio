This, is me.

Life in Nanyang Polytechnic



I first enrolled in Nanyang Polytechnic back in 2022 April through the Polytechnic Foundation Programme (PFP) as a year 0 after scoring an EMB3 of raw 7. My PFP year to me was the most enjoyable academic year of my life as I was able to create new connections and learn things in different ways.



Now in 2023, I am officially enrolled into the Diploma in Information Technology. Though my class has changed, I am still able to meet my PFP friends in school. And I've also made connections with quirky people in my new class.

Co-curricular Activities

I am currently in 5 CCAs, namely Kickboxing, Entrepreneur Club, SIT Club, Mentoring Club and Leo Club. My roles in these CCAs are as follows:

CCAs Description On-Goings
Kickboxing - Fighter-Team A martial arts CCA that I have twice a week on Mondays and Wednesdays. Kickboxing, for me, is not just a sport; it's a way to challenge myself physically and mentally. Apart from the physical aspects, kickboxing has taught me the importance of respect, both for my opponents and fellow practitioners. It's an environment that fosters camaraderie and mutual support, which I find truly inspiring. Currently in preparation for Martial Arts Night (MAN) in September 2023.
Entrepreneur Club - Sub-Commitee As an aspiring entrepreneur, I've constantly sought opportunities to learn, grow, and connect with like-minded individuals. Joining this Entrepreneur Club is a perfect fit for me as it provides an excellent platform to collaborate, exchange ideas, and gain valuable insights from fellow aspiring entrepreneurs. No current on-goings.
SIT Club - Participating Member Being part of this club gives me the opportunity to indulge in my love for tech-related events and activities. From coding challenges to hackathons and everything in between, I'm eager to explore and learn more about the vast realm of IT. In Application for Sub-Commitee Member
Mentoring Club - Participating Member Being part of this Mentoring Club allows me to not only seek guidance from experienced individuals but also offer my support and knowledge to those who might benefit from it. I have always been passionate about helping others achieve their goals, and I find great fulfillment in being a mentor and mentee. Currently mentoring students in Serangoon Gardens Secondary School for their national exams.
Leo Club - Participating Member LEO stands for Leadership, Experience and Opportunity, and the club contributes to society by promoting service activities among the youths in the respective communities. It also unites its members through friendship, fellowship and mutual understanding through service. No current on-goings.
